Hello

We are Aspec*German, a small discord oasis for Aces, Aros, people in the respective spectrums and of course for everyone who is questioning.

On our server we offer the possibility to exchange and find/share resources about these topics among others as well as gender, memes, relationships and more.
Every two weeks we organize meetings that take place in the language chats.
Regional groups also have their place here and hold their meetings with us.

How does one become part of the Aspec*German server? It’s very simple:

  1. Either download Discord for your operating system or use it via your browser
  2. create an account (if you don’t already have one)
  3. then join our oasis using the following invitation code in Discord: HnkcTgX

We are always happy to welcome new people!

Just one note though: almost all of our content is in english, but we sometimes take part or organize international events. For these purposes we will create international/english speaking channels.
